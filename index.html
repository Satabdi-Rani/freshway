About Us

Sparky's solutions are designed to support all participants at every phase of the pre- and post-harvest supply chain. They aim to facilitate growth and minimize risks related to payments, quality, logistics, while providing trustworthy real-time data and effortless access to sourcing from reputable, verified businesses, both domestic and international.
